plebber

bio

Plebber is a techno artist from Copenhagen who has been making an impression in the city's electronic music scene with his unique sound and engaging live performances. His productions are characterized by their intricate drumwork, modified vocal chops and hypnotic melodies, inspired by the european scenes such as Berlin, Vienna and the local Copenhagen scene. Plebber's live performances are known for their intense energy, fast rhythms and changes in genre.
